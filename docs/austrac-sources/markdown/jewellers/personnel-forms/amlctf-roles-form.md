# AML/CTF roles form

Use this form to identify eligible personnel in your business that will fulfill each AML/CTF role.

One person can fill multiple roles. To help streamline your processes, we recommend that your AML/CTF compliance officer also be a senior manager.

### Governing body

Who's eligible:

- the person or group with primary responsibility for governance and executive-level decisions.

What they do:

- set the strategic direction for the AML/CTF program
- appoint key AML/CTF roles
- review and accept compliance reports
- ensure the AML/CTF compliance officer has the authority and resources to carry out their duties.

Typical candidate:

- in most small businesses, this will be the business owner.

### Senior manager

Who's eligible:

- a senior team member who can make or influence decisions affecting the whole or a substantial part of your business.

What they do:

- approve the AML/CTF program and any updates
- decide whether to continue providing services to high-risk customers.

Typical candidate:

- often a senior member of the leadership team, such as a store manager.

### AML/CTF compliance officer

Who's eligible:

- an Australian resident who holds a management level role. They must have sufficient authority, independence and access to the necessary resources and information to fulfill the role.

What they do:

- oversee day-to-day compliance tasks
- ensure the business follows its AML/CTF policies and procedures
- manage complex or high risk matters escalated to them by customer-facing personnel.

Typical candidate:

- a team member with significant influence over business decisions, such as a store manager
- any personnel you wish to assist the AML/CTF compliance officer in completing their duties.

### Other AML/CTF roles, including customer-facing personnel

Who they are:

- frontline personnel who monitor customers for unusual activity and fulfill any other AML/CTF roles.

What they do:

- monitor customers for unusual activity
- identify and escalate high risk or unusual matters to the AML/CTF compliance officer
- fulfill any other AML/CTF roles

Typical candidate:

- salespeople
