Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Pro for free?

Yes! All Pro subscriptions start with a 14-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial at no cost.

Can my team share one account?

Yes. T2C is designed for sole traders and small firms (1-3 people). One login is shared across the business — no per-seat charges.

What's the difference between Pro and the one-time PDF?

Pro gives you full access to all compliance tools (risk assessment, forms, training, etc.) plus PDF generation. The one-time PDF purchase ($149) only gives you the ability to generate the AML/CTF program document — no access to other Pro tools.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime from your subscription management portal. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Is this a legally valid AML/CTF program?

T2C is an educational tool that helps you build your compliance program. The generated documents are based on AUSTRAC's published guidance. You should have your program reviewed by a compliance professional before finalising it. See our disclaimer for details.

How much cheaper is T2C compared to alternatives?

T2C Pro costs $29/month — significantly cheaper than easyAML ($179/mo), HeadStart ($679+ one-time), or engaging a law firm ($2,000-$10,000+). The annual plan ($249/year) saves an additional 30%.
